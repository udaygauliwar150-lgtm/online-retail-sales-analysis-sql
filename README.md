# 🛒 Online Retail Sales Analysis using SQL

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes an online retail sales dataset using **PostgreSQL** to uncover valuable business insights. It focuses on customer behavior, product performance, sales trends, and country-wise revenue through SQL-based data analysis.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Perform data validation and quality checks
* Explore sales and customer data
* Calculate key business KPIs
* Analyze product and customer performance
* Identify country-wise sales trends
* Generate actionable business insights

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Database:** PostgreSQL
* **Language:** SQL
* **Concepts Used:**

  * Aggregations
  * GROUP BY & ORDER BY
  * CASE Statements
  * Common Table Expressions (CTEs)
  * Window Functions
  * Date & Time Functions
  * Ranking Functions

---

## 📊 Key Business Questions

* What is the total revenue?
* How many orders and customers are there?
* Who are the top customers by revenue?
* Which products generate the highest sales?
* Which countries contribute the most revenue?
* What are the monthly sales trends?
* What is the average order value?

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Generated over **£10.6M** in total revenue.
* Analyzed **524K+** retail transactions.
* The **United Kingdom** contributed the highest revenue.
* A small group of customers generated a significant share of total sales.
* Top-selling products accounted for a large portion of revenue.
* Sales showed clear monthly and seasonal trends.
